Onset Localization Study: does the pathway story depend on how we pick l*?

Causal Semantic Pathway Tracing (§22) starts at an onset layer l*. We located it with AUROC; TraceRouter uses an attention-divergence score. This study implements a feasible attention-based onset, compares the two, and tests whether the source-bottlenecked / distributed-relay architecture findings survive the choice.

Models Llama-3.1-8B · Qwen3-0.6B · Llama-3.2-1B Knowledge safety, math, economics, medical, language_hu Artifacts pathways/onset_attention.json, cspt_suppress_*onset_summary.json

1.The question

The pathway pipeline (§22) cuts a knowledge item's source neurons at an onset layer l* and measures what collapses downstream. We chose l* by detector AUROC (first layer crossing 0.70). TraceRouter does not — it uses an attention-based Sensitivity Score. So a fair worry: are the §22 conclusions an artifact of the onset method? This study answers that.

4.Robustness: do the architecture labels survive the onset change?

We re-ran the entire CSPT pipeline (FIS trace + factorial suppression) under the attention onset and re-classified each (model, knowledge) as SB (source-bottlenecked: src alone collapses), DR (distributed-relay: only path collapses, beating magnitude-matched random), none (nothing collapses), or weak (detector too weak to trace).

Stable — the core findings survivesafety (SB in both Llamas, DR in Qwen) and topic_math (DR everywhere) carry the identical architecture under both onset methods, all three models. In particular the headline family finding — safety is source-bottlenecked in Llama and distributed in Qwen — is onset-robust.
Onset-sensitive — and why (a real methodological lesson)language is carried in the earliest layers (peaks L0–L4). When the attention onset places l*=L2, the traced path starts at L3 and misses the carriers, so nothing collapses (label → none/SB). medical's source-bottleneck holds only when cut at L0; cut at L2–L3 the source is no longer a bottleneck (→ none). economics is weak either way. The lesson: onset must sit at or before where a knowledge item's carriers begin. For early-localized knowledge that means L0 — which AUROC-first-crossing happens to pick, while the attention-onset's slightly-later first peak does not.

5.Downstream-K: how many neurons is "the path"?

The path is the Top-kdown FIS neurons per downstream layer. We used kdown=10 — but the paper never specifies a downstream K: its mask is qualitative ("neurons with a high FIS score"), and its one quantitative cutoff (the elbow of the score curve, Fig. 11) is for the WFS source at the onset layer, not the downstream FIS mask. So we test (a) the paper-faithful elbow applied per downstream layer, and (b) a sweep of fixed kdown.

Two orthogonal axes of "distributed"The families differ on both: across depth — Llama gates safety/medical at a concentrated onset source (SB), Qwen spreads every item across layers (DR); and within each layer — Qwen's per-layer carriers are sparse (elbow ~10), while Llama's are broad/diffuse (elbow ~40–60). Qwen is "depth-distributed but per-layer-concentrated"; Llama (where distributed) is "per-layer-diffuse." A sharper structural fingerprint than depth alone.

6.Verdict & implications

  1. The headline conclusions are onset-robust. "Two architectures (source-bottlenecked vs distributed-relay)" and "safety is source-bottlenecked in Llama but distributed in Qwen" hold identically under both AUROC and attention onsets. The §22 family finding does not depend on the onset method.
  2. Per-item architecture labels for medical / language / economics are onset-sensitive and should be stated with that caveat in §22 — they flip with a 1–2 layer onset shift.
  3. Onset placement matters most for early-localized knowledge. If carriers live in the first few layers (language), the onset must be L0 or the downstream trace skips them. This argues for onset = earliest emergence (AUROC first-crossing or SS first-peak), never a global/late peak.
  4. The two feasible signals agree where it counts. Both put safety at L0 in every model and cluster all onsets at L0–L7; the attention lens independently corroborates that economics is not a clean concept.
  5. The downstream-K choice is not a lever (§5). SB/DR labels are identical across kdown = 5–50 and the paper-faithful elbow. The elbow additionally exposes a within-layer sparsity axis: Qwen ~10 (sharp), Llama ~40–60 (diffuse).
RecommendationKeep AUROC first-crossing as the default onset (it correctly captures early carriers and needs no extra forward pass), but report the architecture label for medical/language/economics as onset-sensitive. The faithful SS remains out of reach without a minimal-pair, token-role battery; the relaxed attention-divergence is a useful cross-check but, by peaking slightly later, can miss early carriers — so it is a validator, not a replacement.
CaveatsSingle seed; attention-divergence uses attention entropy as the per-prompt scalar (one of several possible attention summaries); onset by first-local-peak; kfeat=10, ksrc=64, kdown=10 unswept. Re-scoring a detector at zeroed layers retains partial circularity (mitigated by the magnitude-matched controls).
